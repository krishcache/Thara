Purpose

This document stores ideas that are intentionally parked.

The backlog exists to protect focus.

Not every good idea should be worked on immediately.

---

## Parking Principles

An idea may be parked when

- Business value is currently low.
- Required information is unavailable.
- The timing is not right.
- Better priorities exist.
- The return on investment is poor.

Parking an idea is a conscious decision,
not a failure.

---

## Review Frequency

Review the backlog periodically.

Questions to ask

- Is this still relevant?
- Has the priority changed?
- Has new information become available?
- Is now the right time?
